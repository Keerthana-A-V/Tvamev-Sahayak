Our work so far

DHAAGA

Dhaaga, our first project, was a short interview series about toxic positivity. People were asked questions and were requested to answer them. The questions, about the topic ranging from if toxic positivity could be a coping mechanism to being asked to share their experience related to toxic positivity.

PEHCHAAN

Pehchaan was a 1 hour webinar that our team conducted on the topic gender and sexuality. The webinar speaker was Ms Manavi Khurana, who specializes in counselling for LGBTQ+ people.

KIZUTSUITA

Kizutsuita, english for wounded, was an "ART Competition" about the theme of Trauma. Participants were asked to draw, paint, or even make a reel about anything trauma related.

Alfaaz was an open mic fundraiser we organized for world suicide prevention day. Particpants showed off their singing and spoken poetry skills on the virtual stage. All fundings went to SPIF, which is a suicide prevention and counselling NGO.
