Kizutsuita

, english for wounded, was an "ART Competition" about the theme of trauma. Participants were asked to draw, paint, or even make a reel about anything trauma related.Trauma is everywhere. It is part of the human condition to hurt and to get hurt. War, murder, death can be seen every single day. To honor the victims of the Hiroshima Nagasaki bombings, and to make people understand the importance of trauma, we organized this competition.
Here are the winner and runner ups for the event.
