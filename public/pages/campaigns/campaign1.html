Campaigns Dhaaga

“Dhaaga

was our first project, which was a short interview series about toxic positivity. People were asked questions and were requested to answer them. The questions, about the topic ranging from if toxic positivity could be a coping mechanism to being asked to share their experience related to toxic positivity. We selected 10 people from our volunteers circle of friends and family members. It was made sure that the participants were comfortable and if they disagreed to a video call, we gave them the option of an audio interview as well. Questions were simple, precise and easy to answer. Check out the toxic positivity episode below.”
